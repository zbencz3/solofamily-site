SoloFamily

A private parenting journal.
Remember the days. Know the pattern. Keep it yours.

SoloFamily is a quiet record of the days for solo parents — log time with the kids, events, expenses, and free-form notes. Scroll back any day and remember. Export a beautiful monthly PDF when you want one.

Nobody sees your data. No server. No account. No trackers. iCloud sync across your own devices, encrypted by Apple.

What's inside

  • Today view with the split for the month
  • Color-coded calendar
  • Periods with parent + caregiver + sleepovers
  • Events, expenses with photos and receipt scanning
  • Day notes for the small stuff
  • Insights with custom ranges
  • PDF + CSV export (Pro)
  • Face ID lock, daily reminder, Siri shortcuts (Pro)

Pricing

Free for unlimited logging. $29 one-time unlocks Pro (export, Face ID lock, daily reminder, receipt OCR, Siri shortcuts). No subscription.
