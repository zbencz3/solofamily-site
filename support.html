Support

Need help, found a bug, or have a feature request? Two ways to reach me — pick whichever suits:

  • Email (private): zbencz3@protonmail.com
  • GitHub issue (public, good for bugs + feature requests): github.com/zbencz3/solofamily/issues

SoloFamily collects no crash logs or telemetry, so I only hear about problems when you tell me. An honest App Store review also helps — this small app is what supports a solo-parent family.

How do I back up my data?

Settings → Export → pick a range → Export CSV. The CSV includes periods, events, and expenses and can be re-imported via Settings → Customise → Import log file (JSON format; CSV import coming later). You can also export a PDF for a human-readable archive.

Why don't I see Pro features?

If your purchase didn't unlock Pro features: Settings → Pro → Restore Purchase.

iCloud sync

iCloud sync is syncing data across your own Apple devices using your personal iCloud account. Your data is end-to-end encrypted by Apple; SoloFamily does not run any server and cannot read it.

How do I delete a record?

Open it (tap the entry on the calendar or a list) and tap the red Delete button at the bottom. On lists, you can also swipe left to delete.

Does the app share my data with anyone?

No. See the Privacy Policy for full detail.
